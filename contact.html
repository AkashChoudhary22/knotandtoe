Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular digital wallets including PayTM, PhonePe, and Google Pay.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within India typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items with original tags attached. Please see our full return policy document for details.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship internationally to select countries. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination.
